Experiment Name: Verification of Stefan's Law

Procedure:

Steps to be followed are as given below:

1. Make connections to complete the circuit as per the following order:

a. Click on 'Select Voltmeter' button and then click over the blank space to draw the Voltmeter.

b. Click on the 'Select Ammeter' button and then click over the blank space to draw the Ammeter.

c. Click on the 'Select Bulb' button and then click over the blank space to draw the Bulb.

d. Click on the 'Select Battery' button and then click over the blank space to draw the Battery.

e. Click on the 'Connect Bulb with Ammeter' button and then click over the blank space to connect Bulb with Ammeter.

f. Click on the 'Complete connection' button and then click over the blank space to complete the connections.

2. Click on 'Enable Power' Button
3. Increase the voltage from 0 V with an increase of 0.5 V with the help of slider.
4. Click on the 'Set' button to know Ammeter reading corresponding to each voltmeter reading.
5. Enter the values of Voltage and Current in Table 1 corresponding to each observation.
6. Similarly repeat the step 3 and Step 4 to take 10 observations.
7. Decrease the voltage from 10.0 V with a decrease of 0.5 V.
8. Follow step 5.
9. Press 'Click' button. This step will copy V and I values from Table 1 and will calculate log P ang log T values.
10. Plot graph between log P ang log T by clicking on 'Draw graph 1' button.
11. Plot the graph between Rt/Ro and T(K) by clicking on the 'Draw graph 2' button.
12. Now hover the cursor over the graph to find the coordinates of any two points and calculate the slope of the curve manually.
13. Enter the slope in input box to verify the result and percentage error.
